Section 07

Health Disclaimer

Important — please read this section carefully.

Breathwork Pro provides guided breathing exercises for educational, wellness, and personal development purposes. The App is not a medical device, and the content provided within the App is not medical advice.

The breathing techniques included in Breathwork Pro (including but not limited to box breathing, Wim Hof Method-style breathing, 4-7-8, pranayama techniques, CO₂ tolerance training, and breath retention exercises) may not be appropriate for all individuals. Some techniques involve intentional hyperventilation or breath retention which carry inherent risks.

Contraindications

You should not use certain breathing exercises in this app, and should consult a qualified physician before using this app at all, if you have any of the following conditions:

  • Cardiovascular disease, heart conditions, or a history of stroke
  • Respiratory disorders including asthma, COPD, or emphysema
  • Epilepsy or seizure disorders
  • Anxiety disorders, PTSD, or panic disorder (certain exercises may be beneficial but should be introduced under professional guidance)
  • Pregnancy
  • Any condition for which breath-holding or altered breathing may pose a risk

Safety Rules

Never practice breath retention exercises:

  • While driving, cycling, or operating any vehicle or machinery.
  • While in or near water (including baths, pools, or open water).
  • While standing up unsupported, to avoid injury from potential fainting.
  • Alone, if you are a beginner to intensive breath retention techniques.

SmartForage assumes no responsibility for any injury, health consequence, or adverse event arising from the use of breathing exercises or techniques provided in the App. By using the App, you acknowledge these risks and accept sole responsibility for your safety and health.
