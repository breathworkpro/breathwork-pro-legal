Section 01

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Breathwork Pro stores the following data exclusively on your device using local storage (no server-side account required):

  • Session history — records of completed breathing exercises including exercise name, duration, date, and session rating where provided.
  • Streak data — your daily practice streak count and last activity date, used to display your progress.
  • Settings preferences — selected exercise configurations, haptic feedback preferences, theme settings, notification preferences, and audio settings.
  • Challenge progress — completion status for 7-day challenges and in-app goals.
  • Calendar heat map data — aggregated daily session counts used to render your activity calendar.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials (unless you opt in to a future cloud sync feature).
  • Location data.
  • Device identifiers beyond what is technically required for subscription verification (see Section 4).
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data.
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone data.
  • Crash reports sent to third-party analytics services.

Future Cloud Sync (Optional)

We may introduce an optional cloud sync feature in a future update. If and when that feature is launched, you will be explicitly asked to create an account and consent to data storage before any information leaves your device. This policy will be updated accordingly before that feature is released.

Section 02

How We Use Information

All data collected by the app is used solely to provide and improve your in-app experience:

  • Displaying your session history and progress statistics within the app.
  • Maintaining your streak and motivating continued practice.
  • Remembering your preferences so the app behaves the way you configured it.
  • Verifying your subscription status to unlock Pro features (via RevenueCat — see Section 4).

We do not use any collected data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or sale to third parties.

Section 03

Data Storage & Security

Your app data is stored locally on your device using Flutter's secure local storage mechanisms. Data is subject to your device's built-in encryption and security model (e.g., iOS Data Protection, Android Keystore).

Because we do not operate servers that store your personal data, there are no server-side databases to breach. The security of your data depends on your device's lock screen and OS security settings.

We recommend keeping your device OS up to date and using a strong device passcode to protect all locally stored app data.

Section 04

Subscriptions & RevenueCat

Breathwork Pro uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status across iOS and Android. RevenueCat is a third-party SDK that allows the app to verify whether you have an active subscription without the app directly accessing your payment details.

What RevenueCat Receives

  • An anonymous user ID generated by RevenueCat (not linked to your name or email).
  • Subscription status and entitlement data (active, expired, trial) sourced from Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • Platform-level receipt data for verification purposes.

What RevenueCat Does Not Receive

  • Your name, email, or payment card details — these remain with Apple or Google exclusively.
  • Any breathing session data or in-app activity.

Billing, payment processing, refunds, and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) according to their respective privacy policies. We never see, store, or process payment information.

RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Section 05

Health & Biometric Data

Breathwork Pro records breath-hold times and session duration as part of your practice history. This information is considered health-related data in some jurisdictions.

This data never leaves your device. Breath-hold times, session history, and all related health data are stored exclusively in local app storage and are not transmitted to SmartForage, RevenueCat, or any third party.

The app does not integrate with Apple Health (HealthKit) or Google Fit by default. If a future update introduces such integration, it will be opt-in and clearly disclosed.

Breathwork Pro is not a medical device and the data it records should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions. See our Terms of Service for the full health disclaimer.

Section 08

Data Retention & Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of your data at all times.

Deleting Your Data

  • Clear all app data: Navigate to Settings → Privacy → Clear All Data within the app to permanently delete all session history, streaks, preferences, and cached data.
  • Uninstall the app: Uninstalling Breathwork Pro from your device will remove all locally stored data. Note that uninstalling does not cancel an active subscription — subscription management must be done through the App Store or Google Play.

RevenueCat Data Deletion

RevenueCat retains anonymous subscription records as required for billing and fraud prevention. To request deletion of your RevenueCat record, contact us at breathworkpro@outlook.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and we will coordinate with RevenueCat on your behalf.

Retention Period

We do not retain your data beyond the period you use the app. There is no server-side retention because we do not collect server-side data.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Breathwork Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us immediately at breathworkpro@outlook.com and we will take prompt action to delete such information.

Users between the ages of 13 and 18 should review this policy with their parent or guardian. Some breathing techniques in the app (e.g., breath retention) are intended for adults and should not be practiced by minors without parental supervision and medical guidance.

Section 10

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Given the privacy-first design of Breathwork Pro, most of these rights are satisfied by the local-only data model. Specifically:

  • Right to access: All data the app holds is accessible directly in the app under your session history and settings.
  • Right to correction: You can modify settings and preferences at any time in the app.
  • Right to erasure: Use the in-app "Clear All Data" function or uninstall the app.
  • Right to data portability: Session data is currently stored locally. Export functionality may be added in a future update.
  • Right to object to processing: We do not engage in any automated processing or profiling of your data.

To exercise any rights relating to RevenueCat's anonymous records, contact us at breathworkpro@outlook.com.

Section 11

GDPR Compliance (EU & EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies to our processing of your personal data.

Legal Basis for Processing

To the extent that Breathwork Pro processes any personal data (primarily through RevenueCat for subscription verification), we do so on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: Processing your subscription status is necessary to provide the Pro features you have purchased.
  • Legitimate interests: Maintaining anonymous technical records necessary for the reliable operation of the subscription system.

Data Transfers

RevenueCat is a US-based company. When subscription data is processed by RevenueCat, it may be transferred to and processed in the United States. RevenueCat maintains appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR requirements.

GDPR Rights

EU/EEA residents have the right to lodge a complaint with their local Data Protection Authority (DPA) if they believe their data rights have been violated. You can also contact us directly at breathworkpro@outlook.com.
